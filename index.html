About

The Vine Pub & Thai Restaurant

Located at 20 Market Street, The Vine is Maidenhead’s go-to hybrid for authentic Thai cuisine and classic pub hospitality. Under new management, we blend a vibrant community atmosphere—complete with live sports and karaoke—with a menu of homemade curries, vegan-friendly dishes, and traditional Sunday roasts. Whether you’re here for a pint of Brakspear ale or a fiery Pad Thai, our dog-friendly space and sun-trap seating offer the perfect local escape.
